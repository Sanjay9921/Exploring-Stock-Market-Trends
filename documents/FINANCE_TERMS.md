# Finance Terms

* **Broker** – Company that executes trades and safekeeps assets for clients.
* **Client** – End investor (e.g. high‑net‑worth individual).
* **Account** – Container of assets for a client at a broker.
* **Asset** – Financial instrument (stock, bond, ETF, etc.) that can be owned.
* **Asset class** – Category of assets (equity, bond, fund, alternative).
* **Holding / Position** – How many units of a specific asset an account owns.
* **Cost basis** – Original purchase price per unit used for profit/loss calculations.
* **OHLCV** – Open, High, Low, Close, Volume (daily trading data for a ticker).
